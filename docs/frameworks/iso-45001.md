# ISO 45001 Compliance with Gover

ISO 45001 is the international standard for occupational health and safety management systems.

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Full Name** | ISO 45001:2018 |
| **Type** | International Standard |
| **Publisher** | ISO |
| **Focus** | Occupational Health & Safety |

## Purpose

ISO 45001 helps organizations:
- Prevent work-related injuries and illness
- Provide safe and healthy workplaces
- Proactively improve OH&S performance
- Meet legal requirements

## Structure

Based on the ISO High-Level Structure:

| Clause | Topic |
|--------|-------|
| **4** | Context of the organization |
| **5** | Leadership and worker participation |
| **6** | Planning |
| **7** | Support |
| **8** | Operation |
| **9** | Performance evaluation |
| **10** | Improvement |

## Key Requirements

### Leadership (Clause 5)
- Top management commitment
- OH&S policy
- Roles and responsibilities
- Worker consultation and participation

### Planning (Clause 6)
- Hazard identification
- Risk assessment
- Legal requirements
- OH&S objectives

### Operation (Clause 8)
- Operational planning and control
- Emergency preparedness
- Procurement
- Contractors

## Using Gover for ISO 45001

### 1. Add the Framework

1. Go to **Frameworks** → **Add Framework**
2. Select **ISO 45001** from templates
3. Add to your workspace

### 2. Identify Hazards

Use Gover's risk management:
1. Create risks for workplace hazards
2. Assess likelihood and severity
3. Determine risk levels

### 3. Map Controls

Link controls for:
- Safety policies and procedures
- Training programs
- PPE requirements
- Emergency procedures

### 4. Track Compliance

Monitor:
- Requirement coverage
- Incident tracking
- Audit findings
- Improvement actions

## Integration with Other Standards

ISO 45001 integrates with:
- **ISO 9001** — Quality management
- **ISO 14001** — Environmental management
- **ISO 27001** — Information security

All use the same High-Level Structure.
